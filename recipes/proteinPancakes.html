Peanut Butter Chocolate Protein Pancakes

Home

Description

These pancakes are a wonderful high protein breakfast option that is easy and quick to make.

ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Chocolate protein powder
  • PB Powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix together about 50/50 mix of pancake mix and protein/PB powder mix.
  2. Stir in milk untill batter to a consistancy for you desired pancake thickness. Thicker batter makes thicker pancakes and thinner batter makes thinner pancakes.
